FAQ

Common questions.

Is this a real voice interview?

Yes. Alex speaks and listens using ElevenLabs voice AI. You answer out loud, just like a real phone screen.

Does it work for any job?

Yes. Paste any job description and Plumlo builds a custom interview around it in seconds.

What does the debrief include?

An overall score, what you said that landed, what you missed, and specific things to fix — all tied directly to the job description.

Do sessions expire?

No. Your sessions are yours to use whenever you need them.

What's the difference between Starter and Pro?

Sessions. Starter gives you 2, Pro gives you 10. Both include the full interview and scored debrief. Pro works out to $4.90 per session.
